Businesses are increasingly turning to industry companies to rent or lease workspaces, including fully furnished offices, virtual offices, and conference rooms. Renting or leasing office space is a cost-effective alternative to buying commercial property

A virtual office address gives your company a real presence anywhere in the world, without you being there. We can handle your mail and forward it on, take your calls, and help you book a meeting room or office when you’re in town.

At eOffice Admin Services, our client’s best interest come first, and everything we do will be guided by our values and professional ethics. We will ensure that we hold ourselves accountable to the highest standards by meeting our client’s needs precisely and completely. We will cultivate a working environment that provides a human, sustainable approach to earning a living, and living in our world, for our partners, freelance workers and for our clients.

There are times when you don’t need to occupy a physical office, but still need the presence that a virtual address can provide. This is where a virtual office can be invaluable, allowing you to build a business in any number of markets at home or overseas, without having to rent and maintain dedicated office space.

This is why our target market includes:

• Organisations looking for space to hold meetings and events.

• Big Corporates looking for staff relief and outsourcing some of their administrative functions.
